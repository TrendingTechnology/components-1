# ThemeProvider

## When to use

- **ThemeProviders provide theme data to their children.** They wrap styled-components `<ThemeProvider>` internally.
- **Wrap all your root level apps in a ThemeProvider.** Look in `styles/theme.ts` to define your theme config.
